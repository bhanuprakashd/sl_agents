# Messaging Playbook

## Brand Voice

**We are:** Confident, direct, specific, human
**We are not:** Corporate, jargon-heavy, pushy, generic

### Voice Examples

| Instead of... | Say... |
|---|---|
| "I hope this email finds you well" | Jump straight to the hook |
| "We leverage cutting-edge AI to synergize..." | "We help [title] at [company type] do X in Y time" |
| "Would love to connect and share more" | "Worth a 15-min call Thursday?" |
| "Our solution is best-in-class" | "[Customer] cut their [metric] by X% in 30 days" |

## Personas & Value Props

### Persona 1: VP of Sales / CRO
**Top priorities:** Pipeline coverage, rep productivity, forecast accuracy
**Pain points:** Reps spend too much time on non-selling tasks; inconsistent process; poor visibility
**Value prop lead:** "Your reps spend [X]% of their time on admin. We give that back."
**Best social proof:** Revenue attainment improvement, ramp time reduction

---

### Persona 2: Sales Ops / RevOps Manager
**Top priorities:** Process consistency, data quality, tool consolidation
**Pain points:** Manual workflows, CRM hygiene issues, stitching together too many tools
**Value prop lead:** "One workflow to replace the three-tool dance."
**Best social proof:** Time saved per rep per week, CRM data accuracy improvement

---

### Persona 3: Head of Marketing / Demand Gen
**Top priorities:** MQL to SQL conversion, attribution, pipeline contribution
**Pain points:** Leads falling through the cracks, sales-marketing misalignment
**Value prop lead:** "Your leads are good. The handoff is where they die."
**Best social proof:** Conversion rate lift, speed-to-lead improvement

---

### Persona 4: Founder / CEO (SMB)
**Top priorities:** Revenue growth, operational efficiency, doing more with less
**Pain points:** Too many tools, no time to optimize, every dollar matters
**Value prop lead:** "Built for teams that can't afford to waste time."
**Best social proof:** Fast onboarding, ROI within first month

## Hooks That Work

### Trigger-based hooks
- "Saw you just raised your Series B — congrats. Growth at that stage usually means [pain]..."
- "Noticed you're hiring a Sales Ops Manager. Usually means [pain] is becoming real..."
- "Your CTO mentioned [topic] in their recent post — we solve exactly that."

### Insight-based hooks
- "Most [role] at [company type] tell us their biggest challenge is [specific pain]."
- "[X]% of teams your size struggle with [problem] after crossing [milestone]."

### Competitor/market hooks
- "Since [competitor] raised, we've talked to a lot of teams reevaluating their stack."
- "A few of your peers at [similar companies] made a switch last quarter — happy to share what they found."

### Compliment + challenge
- "[Company] is clearly doing [X] well. The teams we talk to at your stage usually hit a wall with [Y] next."

## Sequences

### 3-Touch Cold Sequence

**Touch 1 (Day 1):** Lead with strongest hook + core value prop. Email.
**Touch 2 (Day 4):** New angle — different pain or social proof. Add LinkedIn connection.
**Touch 3 (Day 8):** Short, direct, break-up tone. "Last one from me — just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried."

### Re-engagement Sequence

**Touch 1:** Reference prior conversation + new trigger (funding, news, product update). "Reaching back out because..."
**Touch 2:** Share a relevant piece of content or case study. No hard CTA.
**Touch 3:** Direct ask — "Still the right time to revisit this?"

## CTAs by Stage

| Stage | CTA |
|---|---|
| Cold first touch | "Worth a 15-min call this week?" |
| Follow-up | "Still worth a quick chat?" |
| Warm / inbound | "Happy to send a quick loom — or easier to just hop on a call?" |
| Re-engagement | "Is [pain] still on your radar?" |
| Closing | "Can we get 30 min on the calendar before end of month?" |

---

## What to Avoid

- Opening with "My name is X and I work at Y" — they can see your signature
- Asking for "a quick call" without stating what they'll get from it
- More than one question in a message
- Attachments in cold outreach (spam trigger)
- Overpromising ("guaranteed ROI", "10x your revenue")
- Messages longer than 150 words (email) or 75 words (LinkedIn)
