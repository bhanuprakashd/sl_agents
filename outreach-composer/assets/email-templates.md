# Email & LinkedIn Templates

Use these as starting skeletons. Always personalize before sending.

---

## Cold Email Templates

### Template 1: Trigger-Based (Funding / Hiring / News)

```
Subject: [Company] + [our company]

[First name],

Saw [Company] just [trigger: raised / hired / announced X] — congrats.

Teams at that stage usually hit a wall with [specific pain]. We help [role] at companies like [similar customer] [result achieved].

[Similar company] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe].

Worth a 15-min call this week?

[Signature]
```

---

### Template 2: Pain-Led

```
Subject: [Pain point] at [Company]?

[First name],

Most [their title] I talk to at [company type] are dealing with [specific pain] — especially after [growth milestone or trigger].

We built [product] specifically for this. [Customer similar to them] used it to [result].

Open to a quick call to see if it's relevant?

[Signature]
```

---

### Template 3: Insight-Led

```
Subject: Something we're seeing at [industry] companies

[First name],

We've been talking to a lot of [their role] at [company stage/type] companies lately. The pattern we keep seeing: [insight about their world].

[One sentence on how we help].

[Customer] solved this in [timeframe] — happy to share how.

15 mins this week?

[Signature]
```

## Follow-Up Templates

### Follow-Up 1: New Angle

```
Subject: Re: [previous subject]

[First name],

Following up on my last note. Wanted to share a different angle:

[New pain point or use case relevant to them].

[One-liner social proof].

Still worth a quick chat?

[Signature]
```

### Follow-Up 2: Break-Up

```
Subject: Last one from me

[First name],

I'll stop reaching out after this — just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried.

[One sentence core value prop].

If the timing's ever right, [easy CTA].

[Signature]
```

## LinkedIn DM Templates

### Cold Connection Request Note (< 300 chars)

```
Hi [Name] — noticed [specific thing about them/their company].
We help [their role type] with [pain]. Think there might be a fit.
Open to connecting?
```

### After Connection Accepted

```
Thanks for connecting, [Name].

Saw [specific trigger or detail]. We helped [similar company] with [result].

Thought it might be relevant — worth a 15-min call?
```

### Re-engagement DM

```
Hey [Name] — we spoke [X months] ago about [topic].

Since then, [new thing: feature, case study, relevant news].

Still on your radar?
```

## Subject Line Formulas

| Formula | Example |
|---|---|
| Question about their pain | "Scaling RevOps at Acme?" |
| Competitor / peer reference | "How [similar company] solved [pain]" |
| Trigger acknowledgment | "Re: [Company]'s Series B" |
| Specific result | "From 3 days to 20 min — [their use case]" |
| Mutual connection | "[Mutual name] suggested I reach out" |
| Curiosity gap | "Something we're seeing at [industry] companies" |

---

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending any message:
- [ ] Is the opening hook specific to this person or company?
- [ ] Is the pain point relevant to their role and stage?
- [ ] Is the social proof from a similar company or use case?
- [ ] Is there exactly ONE call to action?
- [ ] Is it under 150 words (email) or 75 words (LinkedIn)?
- [ ] Did you remove all filler phrases ("I hope this finds you well", "just checking in")?
