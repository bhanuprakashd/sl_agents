Research Foundation

RAMP Enhancements

Based on arXiv 2508.11120 — "Towards Reliable Multi-Agent Systems for Marketing Applications via Reflection, Memory, and Planning"

🔁
Reflection Loop
Three-layer quality enforcement: self-reflection gates in all 13 agents, orchestrator 3-point check after every sub-agent call, and a dedicated reflection_agent for high-stakes outputs. Max 2 correction cycles per request.
+28pp accuracy — applied across both teams
🧠
Long-Term Memory
Shared SQLite memory store — both teams read and write. Marketing saves ICP insights that Sales uses in research. Sales saves objections that Marketing uses in campaign messaging. Cross-team learning from every interaction.
+20pp recall — applied to cross-team and cross-session continuity
✅
Output Verification
Tool-level verification: DuckDuckGo results scored for company relevance (confirmed / unverified), Salesforce records confidence-matched before writes, MQL packages validated before handoff to Sales.
Prevents bad data from propagating between teams
