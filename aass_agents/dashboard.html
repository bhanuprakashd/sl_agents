Cross-Team Protocols

Team Handoffs

Structured data contracts passed between Marketing and Sales teams.

🟣 Marketing → Sales MQL Handoff

Triggered when audience_builder produces Tier 1 MQL packages (score ≥70/100). Company orchestrator confirms with user, then passes each MQL to sales_orchestrator as a ready-to-action prospect — research already done.

Company name · domain · size · industry
Contact name · title · LinkedIn URL
ICP score (X/100) + per-dimension breakdown
Intent signal — specific trigger (e.g., "hired RevOps Manager last week")
Pain point to lead with in outreach
Recommended channel + outreach angle
Content they engaged with (if any)
Sales team starts at Step 2 (Outreach) — research phase is already complete.
🔵 Sales → Marketing Win/Loss Feedback

Triggered when a deal closes (won or lost) in sales_orchestrator. Feedback flows to marketing_orchestrator to refine ICP scoring, update campaign messaging, and create content that addresses real objections from the field.

Deal outcome: WON / LOST + stage reached
Deal size · company type · industry
Primary objection that came up
Competitor mentioned (if any)
Content / resources that helped close (WON)
ICP profile (for lookalike audience building)
Marketing action: update ICP / fix messaging / create content
Routes to: audience_builder (ICP update) · content_strategist (case study or objection content) · campaign_composer (messaging fix)
🟦 Research → Sales / Marketing Competitive Intelligence

Triggered when competitive_analyst_agent or research_scientist_agent produces a validated intelligence package. Passes through reflection_agent quality gate before routing. Sales receives battle cards; Marketing receives messaging update briefs.

Competitor name · product comparison · pricing data
Differentiators + weaknesses to exploit
Sales: battle card — objection responses per competitor
Marketing: positioning + messaging update brief
Requires reflection-check before routing. Routes to objection_handler_agent (sales) and brand_voice_agent + campaign_composer_agent (marketing).
🟩 Product → Marketing GTM Auto-Trigger

When product_orchestrator ships a feature or product, it auto-routes to marketing_orchestrator with a structured GTM brief. Marketing launches a coordinated campaign without manual handoff.

product_name · live_url · version
core_features (list) · target_persona
technical_summary — for content_strategist
suggested_campaign_angle · launch_priority
Triggers: campaign_composer_agent (launch copy) · content_strategist_agent (blog/docs) · seo_analyst_agent (keyword targeting)
🔴 QA → Engineering / Product Quality Gates

Triggered when qa_orchestrator completes a test cycle. PASS status notifies the originating team and clears the build for the next stage. CRITICAL DEFECT blocks release and routes back with a structured defect report.

PASS → notify originating team · cleared for next stage
CRITICAL DEFECT → block release · route back to team
defect_id · severity · component · reproduction_steps
test_coverage_report · performance_baseline · security_findings
Routes to: engineering_orchestrator (defect fix) · product_orchestrator (release decision) based on originating team.
🟠 Autoresearcher → All Departments Evolution

The autoresearcher_orchestrator periodically evaluates agent instruction quality, generates hypotheses for improvement, and rewrites agent prompts. The rollback watchdog monitors for regressions and auto-reverts if quality drops.

hypothesis — proposed instruction improvement
target_agent · current_version · proposed_version
evaluation_score · quality_delta · confidence
rollback_trigger — auto-revert on regression detection
Continuously evolves all 60+ agents. rollback_watchdog_agent monitors every deployment and reverts within one cycle if quality signal drops below threshold.

Research Foundation

RAMP Enhancements

Based on arXiv 2508.11120 — "Towards Reliable Multi-Agent Systems for Marketing Applications via Reflection, Memory, and Planning"

🔁
Reflection Loop
Three-layer quality enforcement: self-reflection gates in all 13 agents, orchestrator 3-point check after every sub-agent call, and a dedicated reflection_agent for high-stakes outputs. Max 2 correction cycles per request.
+28pp accuracy — applied across both teams
🧠
Long-Term Memory
Shared SQLite memory store — both teams read and write. Marketing saves ICP insights that Sales uses in research. Sales saves objections that Marketing uses in campaign messaging. Cross-team learning from every interaction.
+20pp recall — applied to cross-team and cross-session continuity
✅
Output Verification
Tool-level verification: DuckDuckGo results scored for company relevance (confirmed / unverified), Salesforce records confidence-matched before writes, MQL packages validated before handoff to Sales.
Prevents bad data from propagating between teams
